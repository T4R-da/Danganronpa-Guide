Prologue - Hello Again, Hope's Peak Academy - Danganronpa 2: Goodbye Despair

1 - Beach
- Make a habit to always examine your surrounding (Press Tab to check which area that available for interaction)
- Use WASD to navigate your surrounding
- Talk to the person in front of you
- Press [R] to exit this area

2 - Bridge

- Here you can press f1 and select "map" to check those 4 areas.
- Feel free to explore
- I started by going right of the screen (Press Q or E for fast traveling)

3 - Airport

- Talk with other 2 students
- Press [R] to exit this area

4 - Rocketpunch Market

- Talk with other 2 students
- Press [R] to exit this area

5 Hotel

- Press Tab to open the map while moving around
- Move forward to the swimming pool area and speak with all the students (there are 3 of them)
- Get inside the hotel (Hotel Lobby)

5B Hotel Lobby

- Talk with 2 other students
- Press [R] and go to "Restaurant"

5C Restaurant

- Talk with another 2 students
- Press [R] to go outside
- Leave the hotel

6 - Ranch

- Talk with another 2 students there
- Press [R] to exit this area

7 - Central island

- Don't forget to check your pet and press clean (Press f1, Then Pet, then spacebar)
- Move left until you meet Jabberwock Park

8 - Jabberwock Park

- Talk with the student
- When you're done talking with everyone, an event should take place
- Head back to the beach (Area 1)

9 - Beach Again

- Talk with Byakuya, an event will play
- Talk with the rest, another event will play
- Talk with Hiyoko
- Press [R] to exit & return to Jabberwock Park
- An event will take place

-Prologue End-
