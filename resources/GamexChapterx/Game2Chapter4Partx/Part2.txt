4. The Final Dead Room

-Go around the room and notice the numbers written on the wall
- Number 3 above Monomi
- Number 9 above the desk ( you can see the line, thus it is 9 not 6)
- Number 7 above the TV
- Number 5 appear if you open the closet
- Grab the hanger from the closet
- Press [R] to close the closet

- Examine the Safe with 4 digits number and enter "3 9 5 7" and press OK to obtain "Camera" and "USB disk"

- Examine The Desk
- Examine the middle drawer to get Pliers
- Examine the right drawer and get uncompress password
- Press [R] to exit

- Right click at "The Plier" & Left Click it on "The Hanger" to obtain "Wire"
- Use "The Wire" on the bed to obtain "Key"
- Use the key on the left drawer at the desk to obtain "Scissor"
- Use "Scissor" on Monokuma Plushie to obtain "Battery"
- Use "Battery" on "Camera"
- Left click "The Camera" to examine it
- Use "USB Disk" on the laptop (try to use it twice until they prompt you to enter the password)
- Enter "SMVEMJS" ( Sun, Mercurius, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn)
- Examine the safe again, this time enter "9875"
- the other device with green digit number is now has its slot open
- Use the USB on the slot
- A scene will play
- Examine the window
- Move your camera to the left until you see another room
- Examine the hatch
