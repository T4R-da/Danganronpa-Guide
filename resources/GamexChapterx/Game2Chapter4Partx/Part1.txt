Chapter 4-1 : Do Ultimate Robots Dream of Clockwork - Daily Life
1. Restaurant
- when the scene done, go to central island and enter the "4th Island"

2. 4th Island
- as usual, i prefer to explore by moving right, so go right to Horror House

3. Horror House

- Not much here really, but feel free to talk and examine things around
- next exit the place and head right to Nezumi Castle

4. Nezumi Castle

- you can pick up Monokuma Pics on the right behind the castle wall (16/30)
- Talk with Nekomaru
- Examine the castle door
- Exit the castle and move right to Rollercoaster

5. Rollercoaster

- grab monokuma pics that ride the roller coaster on the right (17/30)
- Exit Rollercoaster and head to Funhouse

6. Funhouse

- examine the train
- after scene, head back to rollercoaster area

7. Rollercoaster

- Examine the rollercoaster
- after scene you will automatically head back to Fun House
- click the train at the funhouse to progress

8. Strawberry House 3rd Floor

- You will ended up in Strawberry House
- press R to leave the current view
- You will able to move around freely
- now search around and grab monokuma pics (18/30)
- Head down via stairs

9. Strawberry House 2nd Floor

- press tab to open the map
- feel free to explore around, but the goal is to go to Lounge

9. Lounge

- Examine the Phone
- Examine the potrait
- exit the view and head to 1st floor via stair

10. Strawberry House 1st Floor

- Talk with everyone
- Examine The Final Dead Door
- Examine the elevator
- Head to the left and enter Strawberry Hall Door to progress
- examine the grape door
- press [R] to return to 1st floor
- Talk to monokuma

11. Grape House 1st Floor

- Enter Grape house Hall door
- Examine the door
- Talk to Chiaki
- Talk to everyone to hear their report
- after the scene, head to elevator
- then head to strawberry hall door and a scene will follow
- head to your room ( it is near the lounge)

12. Free Time
- Go to lounge beside your room and check the clock
- go to 3rd floor and enter Monokuma Archives
- Check Monokuma golden statue that has grey shadow behind it, get monokuma pics (19/30)
- Go to Strawberry House 2nd Floor and enter Nekomaru Room, You'll see another Monokuma pics hidden behind the desk (20/30) < * Credit to : IGN Wikis >
- Free Time x3

13. Grape Tower
- head down to 1st floor
- go to Grape Hall
- You will get another free timex2
- head to Final Dead Door on Strawberry House 1st floor

14. Grape Tower
- On the next day head back to Grape Tower
- Scene will Play
