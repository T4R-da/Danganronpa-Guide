Chapter 5: Smile at Hope in the Name of Despair - Daily Life

1. 5th Island
- This time you will automatically start from 5th island
- so head right to vendor street

2. Vendor Street
- Pick Monokuma pics (21/30)
- exit area, Head right to Factory

3.Factory
- Enter the metal door
- feel free to examine the factory
- examine the basket full of Monokumas
- Pick Monokuma Pics on top of the basket (22/30)
- Exit the area
- at the entrance of the factory, head to warehouse door to the right

4. Warehouse
- Examine the monokuma pics ( to trigger the event..)
- Exit Factory and head to Military Base

5. Military Base
- Grab Monokuma Pics above the red container to the left (23/30)
- Talk to Sonia
- Leave the area and head to Sea King Industry

6. Sea King Industry
- take a look on your right, you will see Monokuma pics on the opposite platform (24/30)
- Examine the machine at the bottom left
- Examine the robot on the left
- Talk to Kazuichi
- Exit the area
- Keep Moving right until you're back at Vendor Street

7. Vendor Street
- Talk to Nagito
- You will be back at hotel. Head back to your room

8. Hotel
- head to restaurant
- head to hotel's lobby
- talk to Sonia
- Go to 2nd Island

9. 2nd Island
- Head to Ancient Ruins
- Examine the door to get closer
- Examine the electronic panel to the left

10. Free Time x2

11. Your Room
- Speak to Nagito
- Head to Lobby
- A scene will play

12. Hotel
- Head to restaurant
- Talk to Nagito
- Head to Nezumi Castle on 4th Island

13. Nezumi Castle
- Go inside
- Examine the writtings on the floor
- after the event, you will be back to your room and ended the day

14. Hotel
- Talk to Nagito
- After the scene, you will be transported to 4th island
- head to Horror House

15. Horror House
- Enter the door to your right
- Talk to Monomi
- Examine the Monomi Plushies
- Examine the Monitors to your right
- Head to Nezumi Castle & exit afterwards
- Head to Roller Coaster & exit afterwards
- a scene will play
- Now, go to Central Island (You can't teleport this time)

16. Central Island
- Head to jabberwock park
- after the scene, head to 3th island and find Chiaki
- Speak to Chiaki
- Exit and head to 5th Island

17. 5th Island
- Go to Millitary Base
- Talk to Sonia
- Head to Factory

18. Factory
- Examine The Truck
- Examine the laptop on the conveyor belt
- Leave the factory and head to the storage on right
- You will be transported back inside the factory after the scene
- Head to the room on your left
- Examine Red Bottle (Move your camera up)
- A scene will play
- head back to storage
- Examine the back of the room
