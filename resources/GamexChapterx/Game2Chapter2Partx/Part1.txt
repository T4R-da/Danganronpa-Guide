Chapter 2: Sea and Punishment, Sin and Coconuts - Daily Life

1. Restaurant

- Talk to Mahiru
- Press [R] and go outside
- Exit Hotel and go to central island
- Move until you find 2nd Island and enter (It is a gate with Number 2 above it)

2. 2nd Island

- Move Right, you can enter the ruin or keep going to library (There is nothing to do in the ruin at this moment)

3. Library

- You will find Monokuma Pics [6/30] on the upper right
- Speak with everyone
- Examine The Bookshelves to trigger cutscene
- Exit Library and go right to Drugstore

4. Drugstore

- Talk with Mikan
- Move your screen to the right slowly and you can see a small Monokuma Pics [7/30]
- Exit Drugstore and head further right and enter Diner

5. Diner

- Talk with Fuyuhiko
- Exit Diner and at the parking lot, Examine the tunnel to head to The Chandler Beach

6. Chandler Beach

- Follow the road and talk with Ibuki
- You can spot another Monokuma pics on your right [8/30]
- Enter the house in the middle

7. Beach House

- Speak with Kazuichi
- You will move automatically to the Ruin

8. Ancient Ruin

- Talk with Chiaki
- Examine The Circle Door at the center
- Cutscene

9. Hotel
- Head to Restaurant for a scene

10. Free Time x2

11. Jabberwock Park
- Head to Jabberwock Park
- Examine the arcade machine on the right

12. Hotel
- Go to restaurant
- Then go to Old Building's Dining Hall

13. Free Time x2

14. Jabberwock Park
- After The Free Time, Head to Jabberwock Park
- Examine the arcade

15. The Game
- Head to class 1-a
- Keep Talking to everyone
- after cutscene, Go to class 1-b
- after another cutscene, you will automatically head back to your room

16. Hotel
- Go to restaurant

17. Free Time x1
- Go meet a student
- Go meet Kazuichi in the supermarket
- Then head to 2nd island's Diner

18. Diner
- Head inside Diner
- Talk to Kazuichi and cutscene will play
- Head back inside to the diner again
- After another cutscene, Head to the beach
- Head to beach house
