Chapter 2: Deadly Life

1. Beach House

- Received Monokuma file #2
- Examine Bat
- Examine Body
- Examine Anime Girl Mask
- Examine Trash Can
- Examine Shower room

- Examine closet to enter it
Examine the yellow object at the floor while you're inside the closet
Also pick up Monokuma doll at upper leftmost (9/30)
Exit Closet

- Examine Refrigerator
- Talk with everyone

- Recheck the shower room again to enter it
Speak with Akane and examine the window above her
Leave the shower room

- Talk to Chiaki Again
- This will automatically take you to Jabberwock Park

2. Jabberwock Park

- Talk to Chiaki
- Examine the arcade
- Press S 5 times

3. The Game

- Go up to 2nd floor
- Enter Music Room
- Walk Right
- Examine the body
- Examine the window

4. Airport
- After the game, head to airport
- Talk to everyone
- Head back to hotel and go to Victim's cottage

5. Victim's Cottage
- Check Envelope on the bed
- Get Monokuma Pics (10/30) on the window behind the TV
- Leave the room and head back to Beach House

6. Beach House
- On your way there, speak to Nagito to enter the house
- Talk to everyone in the beach house
- Talk to Nagito again to trigger cutscene
- Head to Monokuma Rock
- Examine the Mountain
