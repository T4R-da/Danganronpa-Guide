Chapter 1: Destination Despair - Daily Life

1. Hotel

- Enter your room (You can find it from the map, press "tab") for a cutscene

2. Hajime's Room

- Find one at the upper-right side , the one with Monokuma picture (1/30)
- Press [R] to exit, Head to Hotel Lobby and Press [R] to go to Restaurant
(Make a habit to always talk to everyone and checking around)

3. Restaurant

- Talk To everyone
- Talk to Byakuya to progress
- Exit and leave the hotel
- Move left to Rocketpunch Market

4. Rocketpunch Market

- Use WASD to find Monokuma picture near vending machine (2/30)
- Feel free to check the vending machine
- Press [R] to exit and move further left to Airport

5. Airport

- Find Monokuma Picture at the baggage section (3/30)
- Press [R] to exit and move further left

6. Beach

- In this beach there is a vending machine (MonoMono Yachine) that works just like the old DanganRonpa 1
-Press [R] to exit and head left to Central Island and go to Jabberwock Park

7. Jabberwock Park

-click at the black object in the center to trigger cutscene

8. Free Time

- Throughout this game, you will be given a "Free Time" where you can talk with fellow students to learn more about them and eventually unlock a new "Hope Fragments" which is necessary to unlock skills
- You can bond with other students by simply talk with them and give them presents that you can obtain via Vending Machine at the Rocketpunch Market or MonoMono Yachine at the beach.
- You can check students location by opening map (Press F1 and Map)
- I will not cover the student interactions in this guide since i'm gonna let you explore by your own. However, i highly recommend you to use this "Free Time" instead of skipping it
- Make sure to buy a skill from Nanami. In order to do that, press f1, select report card and select Nanami Icon. If you still confuse about with skill to choose, you never go wrong by going with Vocabulary & Ambidextrousness
- Also don't forget to check your pet too
- And finally, make a habit to save your game frequently (Press f1, system and save)

9. Jabberwock Park
- After the announcement head to Jabberwock Park
- Talk with Fuyuhiko Kuzuryu

10. Restaurant
- Exit your room and head to restaurant and scene will play

11. Free Time x2

12. Old Building

- After the announcement, leave your room and go to the wooden old building to the left beside the hotel lobby
- Press Tab To open the map and enter the room beside the toilet (Office)
- Find Monokuma Picture (4/30)
- Feel Free to search other room before entering the Dining Hall

13. Dining Hall

- Talk To Everyone
- Examine The Dishes
- Examine the Iron Bolted Window behind to progress

14. Front Hall

- Talk to Byakuya
- Enter Kitchen

15. Kitchen

- Examine Knife Set
- Examine Equipment List
- Examine Dishes
- Talk with Byakuya

16. Front Hall

- After the scene, exit the building and go outside
- another scene will play

17. Dining Hall

- Search it !

-End-
